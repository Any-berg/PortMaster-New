## Controls

| Button | Action |
|--|--| 
|Select|Menu|
|Start|Select Option|
|A|Switch Weapon|
|B|Dash|
|X|Activate|
|Y|Press for Sword Attack/ Hold to Heal|
|Dpad/L Analog|Movement|
|R Analog|Aim|
|R2|Shoot Gun/Press to Fire Hold to Charge|
