## Controls

| Button | Action |
|--|--| 
|D-pad / Left stick|Move, aim your tongue left, right, or up.|
|A|Use your tongue (the longer you hold the further it goes!)|
|B|Activate your bubble (if you have it)|
|Start|Pause|
