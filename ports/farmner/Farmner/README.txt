Controls for FarmNer:

" E " to Attack (Hold).
" WASD " to Move & " SHIFT " to Sprint. 
" Left-Click " to Interact.
" Right-Click " to Turn Flashlight On/Off.
" H " or " Q " to Heal.
" Escape " to Pause.
