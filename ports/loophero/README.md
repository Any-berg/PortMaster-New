## Controls

| Button | Action |
|--|--| 
|A|Select or confirm|
|B|Go back or close menu|
|X|Open build menu or inventory  |
|Y|View hero stats or upgrades    |
|R2|Switch between build cards or inventory tabs    |
|L1|Stats|
|L2|Hide controls|
|L3|Speed control|
